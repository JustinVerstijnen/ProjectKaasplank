Welcome to my blog section. In my blog posts, I describe the steps I took to make things work and/or explain how it works. The navigation on this page is relatively easy and I will do my best to explain all features of the blog to you as a manual.

## 1. Light/Dark mode

The website has a native light mode and dark mode in the top right corner. This makes the webpage a better experience for low-light environments.

## 3. Tags

I also use tags on posts to classify what type of post it is. Posts can have multiple tags. At the moment of writing I use these tags:

- **AI Generated Content**: I like an open and honest approach on AI. Everyone uses it, and posts with this tag contain AI generated content. Review my [Terms and Conditions](https://justinverstijnen.nl/blog/terms-conditions/#8-use-of-artificial-intelligence-ai) on AI usage on my website, tools and social profiles
- **Concepts**: In these posts, I explain throrougly how an solution or protocol works
- **Step by Step Guides**: In these posts, I will execute tasks step-by-step
- **Tools and Scripts**: In these posts, I share a tool or script which I find useful. These contains tools and scripts both made by me and not made by me and always refer to the original owner/creator
- **Try Outs**: In these posts, I will try something out what may not be best practice but are great for learning and exploring

You can find a list of all tags [Here](https://projectkaasplank.justinverstijnen.nl/tags/) or at every page in the blog section.


## 4. Table of Contents

On the right on every post, there is a live Table of Contents section. Using this saves a lot of valuable screen-space. This will scroll with you, which makes navigating through different steps a lot easier.

Every heading in the article has a blue Hashtag which is a hard link to a specific heading. Great for references or sharing a specfic part of the article. I use them as well in different posts to navigate you through an earlier or later section.
